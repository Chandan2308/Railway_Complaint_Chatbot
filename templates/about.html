About RailMadad AI Chatbot

Making railway complaint filing faster and easier.

What is RailMadad AI?

The RailMadad AI Chatbot is designed to help railway passengers quickly file complaints regarding issues they face during their journey. It analyzes railway-related complaints from Twitter and other sources, categorizes them based on priority, and files them efficiently with RailMadad for resolution.

How to Use?

  1. Type your complaint in the chatbot on the homepage.
  2. The chatbot analyzes your complaint and assigns a category.
  3. Your complaint is then stored and can be reviewed by railway authorities.
  4. You will receive a response regarding your complaint acknowledgement.
